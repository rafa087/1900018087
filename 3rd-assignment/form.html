    • HNYU PY Course

    Hnyu py is a language training place in Yogyakarta. Hnyu py is also committed to playing a role in efforts to improve language competence broadly

    Description

    Mandarin is designed to train participants to understand grammar in Mandarin, introduce kanji, pinyin (Chinese writing in Latin letters), shēng diào (intonation) so that participants are able to communicate both verbally and in writing effectively. Also given a brief introduction to culture from China for example: history, traditional clothing, special food, literature, and so on.
